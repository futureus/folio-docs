Brand logos

Loitered throughout the app your logo will be visible, it's important that your logo is clear, not cluttered and in keeping with the style o the app.

The top bar where your brand will be located is always dark grey fading to black, so make sure your logo is bright and works with a dark background. A standard theme in FutureFolio based apps is the use of solid white icons and logos on the top bar, it's suggested that you follow this.

As for your artwork files, you will need to create two - four as with the app icon:

  • iPad logo (file name: logo.png) - Your main iPad logo, it needs to be 32 pixels tall, the width is variable, but please refrain from extremely long images as it may interfere with other design elements.
  • iPad logo retina (file name: logo@2x.png) - As with all retina images, this image needs to be identical only twice the size. This image needs to be 64 pixels tall.
  • iPhone logo (file name: logo-iPhone.png) - Like the iPad logo this file needs to be 32 pixels tall. Remember that the iPhone is a lot slimmer than an iPad, you may want to make the image somewhat thinner to fit correctly in the limited space available on the device.
  • iPhone logo retina (file name: logo-iPhone@2x.png) - The same image as the non-retina version, this time it's 64 pixels tall.
