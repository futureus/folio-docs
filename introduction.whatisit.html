What is FutureFolio?

FutureFolio is a next-generation magazine delivery platform for tablets and smartphones, offering a wide spectrum of functionality that can fit a variety of use cases.

The platform is made up of three parts: a store front, digital replicas and interactive editions. FutureFolio allows you to mix and match digital replicas and interactive editions whenever and however you need within a single store front, which allows you to build a shared subscriber base regardless of what content you deliver.
