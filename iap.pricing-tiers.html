Pricing Tiers

The app pricing is structured in tiers, in increments of $1 (currently 69p). So tier 1 is 69p, tier 2 £1.49 etc. Your app could also be free. You can have a maximum of 3 subscription options in addition to the single issue price. Apple will occasionally make an adjustment to the tier structure based on the currency exchange rates. Tier changes

Apple occasionally changes the tiers, and occasionally adjusts for currency exchange fluctuations against the US dollar. Do check the up to date list within your iTunes connect account before making pricing decisions.
