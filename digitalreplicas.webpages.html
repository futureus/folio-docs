Adding web pages

Linking to a webpage couldn't be easier. Simply create a hyperlink inside your Desktop Publishing Suite and enter the full user including http://. Tapping these links will open the URL via Safari.

Automated weblinks

If you type a website URL inside your copy, most DTP applications will convert these into links for you when you export to PDF. If they don't by default, check your export settings for an option along the lines of "Convert URLs to hyperlinks".
