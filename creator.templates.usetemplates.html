Using templates

You may not know this, but if you've every used FutureFolio Creator, then you've actually used a template. When you add a page it will pop up with a list, at first it will just say "Blank", this is the default template. As you create them this list will contain all your templates, simply tap the one you want and a page of that style will be added to the bottom of your issue.

Templates are not unique to that publication, regardless of what issue you created it in. You can add that template page to any publication you wish, they are global to your installation of FutureFolio Creator

Adding a template page

Templates are global, images are not

Templates may have been created with images from the publication they were created for, if you don't have those files in your issue directory then you will get multiple warnings about missing images.

Template: missing image warning

The alert that will display if your template is missing one or more image assets.
