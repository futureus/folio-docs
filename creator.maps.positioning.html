Positioning

Map options

When you add a new map layer, the map is by default centred on the user's current position. In most cases you will want to move the map such that it is centred on whatever is your region of interest. This is easily achieved by dragging the map inside the New map layer screen. Press the Map button to add a map layer, and then press the cog icon. Press and drag the map to reposition it over your region of interest. The big slider in the middle of the options section sets the initial zoom level on your map.

Current position inside Creator

Creator runs inside the iPad simulator, which has no way of adequately determining your actual position. If you wish, you can set the current position using the Location menu option for the iOS Simulator, under Debug.
