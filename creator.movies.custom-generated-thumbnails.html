Custom vs Generated Thumbnails

Movie thumbnail options

A thumbnail is a representative image for a movie that will be shown before the movie is played. Creator allows you to either specify a thumbnail image of your choice as a file, or to pick a frame at a specified time. To use a specific thumbnail image, enter its filename in the Thumbnail text field. In order to pick a frame from the movie as the thumbnail, enter a time in the seconds field and click the Generate thumbnail at time button. See "Movie thumbnail options"
