App Metadata

The Apple iTunes App Store displays an unique page for each app, this is your window to the world and the page that will make or break your app. The data and images on this page are handled by your provided app metadata and uploads.

In this section we will cover some essential tips, tricks and guides on making the most of your metadata while still following the Apple guidelines and maximising the possibility of your app getting past the Apple review process that each app has to go through.
