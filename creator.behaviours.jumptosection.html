Jump To Section

This behaviour will jump to the first page of the section named as its parameter. For example, if you have a Tutorials section that has a variety of pages inside, use "Jump to Section" and set "Tutorials" as its parameter to jump to whichever page is first in the Tutorials section.

Parameter: the name of the section to show.
