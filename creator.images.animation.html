Animation

The animation image layer type is used to display a series of images which automatically play back in sequence.

Specify the images to display in the animation with the Filenames button (see "Importing Filenames").

Once you have selected your sequence of images, you can adjust the speed at which they play back with the Speed control. This is specified in seconds, so that a speed of 1 will result in an image change every one second.

Specify the number of times to loop playback of the image sequence with the Times to play control. A setting of 1 will cause the images to cycle through once and then settle on the final image. A setting of 2 will result in two cycle-throughs before settling on the final image, and so on. Leaving this control blank will result in an infinitely repeating cycle - the images will keep cycling through for as long as the user remains on that page.

The final setting for image animations is the Cross fade toggle. Set this to "ON" to make the images fade from one to the other; set it to "OFF" to make them do a clean cut from one to the next.
