Background Image and Scale

Use the Background button to select the image to use as a background. The image will be displayed to the layer size, maintaining the image's aspect ratio.

As in the above example ( "Annotation layer dialog"), you may wish your image to be smaller than the annotations layer, to allow space for the information boxes around the edge. Use the Image scale slider to scale your image within the layer.

Changing image scale

When you change the scale of your image with the Image scale slider, any pins which you have already added will remain in the same position relative to the layer, not the image.

You should therefore decide on an appropriate image scale before performing any detailed alignment of pins and info boxes to avoid having to realign them.
