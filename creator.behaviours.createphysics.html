Create Physics Force

This behaviour will create a physical force on the page, as if an explosion had gone off where the user tapped. You can control the strength of the force through the behaviour's parameter: start with a value of 100, then adjust it upwards or downwards to strengthen or weaken the effect.

For this behaviour to work, physics must have been enabled on the page.

Parameter: the strength of the force to create.
