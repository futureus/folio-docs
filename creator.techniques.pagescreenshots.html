Custom Page Screenshots

FutureFolio Creator will automatically take screenshots of your page when you finish editing them, and these will be used in the table of contents and page scrubber if you use them.

If you have a more advanced layout where the automatic screenshot of the page is unsuitable, you can create your own in one of two ways:

  • Run the page in the simulator, manipulate it until you're happy, then choose Hardware > Shake Gesture from the iOS Simulator menu. FutureFolio will catch this gesture and recreate your page screenshot based on the current layout of the page.
  • Overwrite the existing screenshot with your own hand-made one. Page screenshots are stored under the meta directory with a unique filename.

Do this last!

FutureFolio regenerates your page screenshot whenever you change the page in Creator, so if you intend to customise page screenshots make sure it is the last thing you do otherwise your changes will be overwritten the next time you modify the page.
