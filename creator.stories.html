Stories

This chapter covers the use of Stories. A story is a piece of text, for example an article. Textual content can be added to a story layer in a number of different ways, either typed in directly, or by referencing a file. Stories can be styled using style sheets, or directly in the story editor interface. A story layer is added by clicking the New Story Layer button as indicated by "Add new story layer".
