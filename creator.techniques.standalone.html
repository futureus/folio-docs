Standalone Issue

FutureFolio has built-in support for standalone issues, which will cause a single issue to load and be shown when the app starts, rather than showing a store front where a user can purchase individual issues.

Standalone issues look and work for all intents and purposes like standalone apps: only one issue can be shown, and as far as the user is concerned the issue is just an app like any other.

Standalone issues must be submitted to Apple by the Future Apps Team, but you create them like any other issue: just produce it until you're ready, then hand it over to the Apps Team for finishing up.
