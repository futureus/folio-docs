Play a Sound (Mixed)

If you have included a sound file in your issue directory, this will play it when the user activates the behaviour. This will not stop sounds that are currently playing, so be warned that it might get noisy if multiple sounds are playing simultaneously. Supported sound formats are MP3, AAC, WAV and AIFF.

Parameter: the filename of the sound to play, e.g. mysong.mp3.

Sounds must be local

Sounds cannot be streamed; they must be played locally.
