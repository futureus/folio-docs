Single Issues

Single issues need to follow Apple's pricing tiers, but may vary from issue to issue. Pricing can also be changed such that a new issue is initially priced higher/lower for a period of time for promotional reasons. Several models exist in terms of pricing strategy. Some magazines have been successful in combining a high single issue price with a heavily discounted, short subscription term, say a 30 day term costing significantly less than the cost of a single issue. See "Pricing Tiers" for details on the pricing tiers.
