Send An Email

This will show an email window over the current page, prefilled with a recipient and optionally a subject.

Parameter: the name of the person who should receive the email, eg hello@futurenet.com. If you follow this recipient with a comma, you can also specify the subject of the email. For example: "hello@futurenet.com,This is a test email".
