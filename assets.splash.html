App splash screen

As your app loads a full screen splash screen is displayed. This image is entirely custom, you can display anything you wish here as long as it doesn't break any of Apple's terms and conditions or guidelines. The retina version needs to match the non-retina image also.

  • iPad splash portrait (file name: Default-Portrait.png/Default-Portrait@2x.png) - Standard: 768x1024 Retina: 1536x2048
  • iPad splash landscape (file name: Default-Landscape.png/Default-Landscape@2x.png) - Standard: 1024x768 Retina: 2048x1536
  • iPhone splash portrait (file name: Default.png/Default@2x.png) - Standard: 320x480 Retina: 640x960
