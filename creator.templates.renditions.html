Page renditions

Pages and all their associated layers can be recreated as different renditions quickly, without having to copy the content manually.

To create a new rendition from an existing page, select the page from the pages section and then click on the rendition type you require. Creator will present a list of the current page renditions to choose from, selecting a rendition from this list will then create a new rendition with all the assets of the current rendition. All the layers will be copied into the new rendition and respect their previous layout hierarchy, however some layers may be placed outside of the visible page and may require re-positioning.

All associated behaviours, animations and effects will be copied when using this feature. Hence, care must be taken when creating renditions for different devices, since some features may not be available on the tablet device which are on the phone, and vice versa.
