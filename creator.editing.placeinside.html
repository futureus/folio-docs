Place Inside

Layers can be grouped inside box layers by using the Place Inside button. First, choose the layer you want to change, then click the Place Inside button, and finally click the box layer on the canvas that you want to use as the container. Alternatively you can select the layers from the table.

To remove a layer from a box, click the layer, click Place Inside, then click a blank area on your canvas.

You can also use the left hand layer list to place your layer inside the desired box with greater accuracy. Should you click on an item that is not a box layer, it will automatically place it inside that layer's encapsulating box.
