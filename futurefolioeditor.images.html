Editing Images and Movies

To edit an image or a movie layer in FutureFolio Editor, double-click on the layer name to bring up the editor window. For images, your filenames are separated by commas; for movies, there is only one filename field for the movie, plus another one for the thumbnail.

You can change the values just by typing in the boxes; to save your changes, press Cmd+S or close the window and follow the prompts.
