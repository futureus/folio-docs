Playing audio

FutureFolio supports the following audio file types: .wav, .caf, .mp3, .aif and .aiff. To add an audio file, include it in your issue archive and create a link in the style of: media://myAudioFile.mp3

As for compression, again this is down to each individual publication, just as long as the output matches one of the supported types mentioned in this section. Please do however bear in mind the impact large files will have on your overall issue download size.
