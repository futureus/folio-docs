Setting JPEG quality

The build process copies your assets over into an issue archive, and it also compresses your JPEG image files. This compression can be the difference between a 500MB issue and a 300MB, so getting the right balance is key to creating an optimal issue.

To start your build, open the finished issue and in the Issue Editor tap the build button. This will bring up the Build Settings window that contains a JPEG Quality slider, a Partial Build switch, and the Start Build button.

We recommend that you start with 50% JPEG quality, and in most cases this compression level is sufficient – we suggest you test the results and adjust the quality only if the results are poor or if your issue size is too large. Settings outside of our recommended quality range will be coloured red. If you're considering using very high quality levels, please refer to the Optimising section ( "Optimising your Issue") for further details.
