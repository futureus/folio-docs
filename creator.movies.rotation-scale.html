Rotation and Scale

The Rotation and Scale sliders allow you to fine-tune the position and orientation of a movie. Rotation sets the angle of the movie from 0 (level) via 180 (upside down) to 360 (back level) degrees whilst maintaining full playback controls. Scale allows you to scale the movie down from 100%. See "Rotation and Scale" for an example.
