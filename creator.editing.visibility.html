Layer visibility

Layers are visible when they are created, which means they are visible to the user and will receive taps when the user touches the screen. Although layers can be hidden by adding as a behaviour, such as when the reader has tapped on a button, you can also hide them initially so that they start life as invisible.

To toggle a layer's visibility, click the eye button next to it in the layers list. If the layer is a box and contains other layers, they will all be invisible.

Show single layer

Hidden layers will be affected by the show single layer behaviour.
