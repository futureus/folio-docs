Viewing in FutureFolio Viewer

You can preview your publications in action using the FutureFolio Viewer app available for free on the iTunes App Store (Search for FutureFolio Viewer). This sections shows you how to import, share and test your issues with ease.

If you are using a digital replica that has a custom password attached to it, FutureFolio Viewer will prompt you to enter the password before continuing. This password will be saved, so you won't be asked for it again. Note: the password will be displayed in clear text to avoid typing errors.
