Locked Mode

Once a page has been laid out exactly how the designer wants it, the canvas can be locked by tapping the Lock button – this will prevent accidental changes from happening to the page.

Locked mode is designed to stop changes to the layout of the page, not its structure. That is, a picture on the canvas can't be moved, but a production editor can change its contents to different pictures.

Locked mode can be toggled by tapping the Spacebar.
