Floating layers

Long pages add a whole new dimension to your layouts. As the page now scrolls the elements on the page move with it, remaining in the positions you stipulated. However you can change this so that layers are fixed on the screen and don't move or move at a slower or faster rate.

Using floating layers you can create parallax scrolling effects, create a static HUD style interface or even reverse the scroll direction.

To make your layer float, select the layer, tap the settings cog and in the Layer Settings window tap the Behaviour segment. in the Behaviour tab you will see "Floats over page". The number you enter here stipulates how slow in percentage you wish the layer to move, so enter 1 - 99 to slow down the scroll speed, 100 to set it as a static non-moving layer (100% slower) and over 100 to scroll in reverse. Zero is the default value, this is zero slow down and thus a normal scrolling object.
