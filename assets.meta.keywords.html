Mastering keywords

The second biggest factor that affects your search ranking are your app keywords. In 100 characters you can stipulate as many comma separated keywords as you wish, the character count includes all comma characters and spaces, so be selective and pick the most common words that you wish to rank for. Also remember that all keywords have to be checked by Apple.

There's no way to "game" the keywords by duplicating the same word, duplication are merely ignored.
