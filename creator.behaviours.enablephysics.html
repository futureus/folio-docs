Enable Physics

This will activate physics on your page, which means all objects that have "Responds to physics" enabled will fall to the ground and bounce as if your page had gravity.

If you have layers that overlap on your canvas, enabling physics may cause them to behave strangely as they try to resolve their overlap.

Parameter: not used.

Not just for Christmas

Once enabled, physics cannot be disabled for that page unless a user browses away a couple of pages and returns.
