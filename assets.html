Asset requirements

Every app on the iTunes App Store has a number of required assets such as an app icon, a launch splash screen, app name, description and other data. Together these requirements make up your app's app store entry and stipulate where it's located, how well it is found and the impact on the end user.

Once your details and images have been created you will need to send them to your account manager for inclusion and usage.
