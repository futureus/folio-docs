What's in this guide

This user guide documents the entire FutureFolio system, so it's very likely that only parts of this guide will be applicable to the average reader.

The five sections of this guide are:

  1. Digital Replicas - this is discusses how our PDF page turner technology works, and explains how to embed multimedia into your issues.
  2. Interactive Editions - this documents our custom issue creation tool, which is used to create advanced, tablet-optimised reading experiences.
  3. Hybrid Editions - this shows how to combine digital replicas and interactive editions into a single issue.
  4. In-app purchases - all you need to do about how your magazines are sold, pricing and delivery.
  5. Asset requirements - the complete list of multimedia files you'll need to provide and what format they should have.
