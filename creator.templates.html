Templates

Templates aren't uncommon in the publishing world and FutureFolio Creator is no different. You can easily save time and effort by creating templates of re-occurring pages and issues. This will improve your workflow dramatically.

In this section we cover:

  1. Using templates - Don't just use the blank template, save some time, use a pre-built page.
  2. Creating templates - Save time by creating generic templates of your pages that can be added in a snap.
  3. Converting to templates - Quickly create alternate renditions, layouts and designs by transferring a page between templates.
  4. Creating issue templates - The hidden, but handy way to instantly set up future issues.
