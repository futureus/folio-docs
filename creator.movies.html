Movies

This section covers the use of movies in Creator. Movies can be added in several ways, both streamed in from a remote web server, or bundled inside the issue itself. Movies can be used as part of a slideshow, but Creator also allows the designer to introduce a dedicated movie layer and set its size, orientation, and configure other aspects of its presentation, such as the representative still to be used as a thumbnail. The iPad and iPhone can only play a handful of movie formats. See for details. A movie layer is added by clicking the New Movie Layer button as indicated by "Add new movie layer".
