Supporting renditions in your issue

To support multiple or an alternate orientation you will need to globally enable that orientation or device in the issue. Enabling a rendition is incredibly simple: In your issue, tap the settings cog button, this will open the Issue Settings window. At the bottom tap the device and orientations you wish to support. Those highlighted in red are supported.
