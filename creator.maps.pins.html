Set pin options

You can drop custom pins at locations of your choice on a map. Pins can be used to highlight a particular point of interest, and you can add a custom title and even behaviours to pins that are triggered when the user taps them. In order to place a pin, click the blue round button with a plus sign in it in the top right corner. See "Add a pin". You can drag the pin around the map to place it in the correct position. To set the options for a given pin, click it so it turns green. You can now move it, and the options section at the bottom becomes active. Here you can set title, subtitle and a behaviour associated with the pin, as illustrated in "Set pin options". See also "Behaviours".
