Customising your Library Cover

By default, FutureFolio will use the first page of your issue for its cover. If you want to have a custom cover, you should copy the following files into your issue directory:

  • page-0.jpg - your cover, sized no greater than 181x256
  • page-0@2x.jpg - your cover, sized no greater than 362x512
  • page-0~iphone.jpg - your iPhone cover, sized no greater than 181x256 (optional)
  • page-0@2x~iphone.jpg - your iPhone cover, sized no greater than 362x512 (optional)

On iPhone, page-0.jpg and page-0@2x.jpg will be used if you don't provide the optional iPhone-specific images.
