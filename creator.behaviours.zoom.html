Zoom Image

When attached to an image, this allows the reader to tap the image and see it full screen. The full-screen image will be placed onto a black background to dim the page behind, and, if it's a slideshow, animation or 360, will retain its interactivity.

All zoomed images can be dismissed by the reader by tapping the picture.

Parameter: the colour to show behind the zoomed image, defaulting to black. This should be specified as a HTML colour code in the format RGBA (Red, Green, Blue, and Alpha). Examples: 100% black is #000000ff, 100% white is #ffffffff, 50% black is #00000080, a mostly opaque yellow is #ffe700bb.
