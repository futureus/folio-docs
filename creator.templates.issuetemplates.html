Creating issue templates

Most likely you will want to template your entire issue once you've created one. This is very simple to do. First browse to your EditingIssues folder, located in /Simulator/EditingIssues/ and copy that to a folder named IssueTemplates inside your templates directory: /Simulator/Settings/. Now when you tap "Add new issue", you will see that template issue in your selection list.

Creating a new issue from a template will copy all images and assets from that template directory into the new issue directory, so you can include all the constant graphics you need for a fast setup.
