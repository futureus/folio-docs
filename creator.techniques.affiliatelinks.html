Affiliate links

If you are sending someone to the iTunes Store, App Store or Amazon, it is strongly recommended that you wrap the URL in an affiliate link so that your link will generate additional income. Note: the amount of income varies depending on the kind of item sold, but all affiliate links continue generating income for other purchases the user makes, even though you didn't link them to it.

To launch an affiliate link when someone taps on an image:

  1. Create your image, and add a new behaviour.
  2. Choose "Load a URL Internally" or "Load a URL Externally" and enter your URL into the parameter box.
  3. Tap the Toolbox icon in the bottom-left corner of the screen and choose either iTunes or Amazon.
  4. When prompted, enter your affiliate ID. This will be remembered for the next time you use this.
  5. When you tap OK, your URL will be converted to an affiliate URL..
