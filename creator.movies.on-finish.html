On Finish

When a movie has finished playing, its default behaviour is "Reset" which means it will return to being the preview thumbnail for the movie with a play button overlaid. You can customise this behaviour from the movie settings window by choosing one of the following options:

  1. Hold - The movie will remain on its last frame..
  2. Loop - The movie will repeat indefinitely.
  3. Hide - The movie layer will be hidden to reveal whatever is behind it.

Using "Hold" with cover lines

If the last frame of your movie is your magazine cover complete with cover lines, using Hold not work quite as you want because movie compression often sacrifices accurate colour values in order to achieve a smaller file size. If you use Hold, please check the final video on an iPad to ensure it has sufficient colour fidelity for your purposes.
