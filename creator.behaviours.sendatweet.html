Send a Tweet

This will open the iOS Twitter client over the current page.

Value: use this field to pre-compose a tweet, or leave blank if you want your user to compose their own. Remember that you are limited to 144 characters for the body of the tweet.

The iOS Twitter client will handle potential issues such as the user not having a Twitter account or being offline, so you can use this feature safely.
