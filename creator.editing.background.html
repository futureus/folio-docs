Background pictures

You can place a picture behind all the objects on your page by using a background picture. This should be the same aspect ratio of your page, otherwise it will appear stretched.

To set a background picture, click the Background Picture button, then select from the list of pictures in your issue directory. You can remove an existing background picture by selecting "No background image" from the list.

It is more efficient to use background images than to place a full-page image layer containing the same image.
