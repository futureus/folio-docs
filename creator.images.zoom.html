Zoom

Most iOS device users are used to pinching to zoom, this layer type enables the user to do just that inside your digital publications.

Use the Filenames button to specify your full size image (see "Importing Filenames").

To set the maximum zoom level, enter how many magnification levels you wish the user to be able to apply in the Zoom levels entry box. This number is in magnification levels based on the size of the layer, so 2 would be a 200% zoom on the size of the layer.

You can set an initial zoom level that the image will be zoomed to by entering a magnification level followed by a comma and the maximum zoom level. (eg: "2, 4" - initial zoom, maximum zoom)

There are three scrollbar styles you can use with your zoomable image layer, just type the one you wish to use in the Scrollbars entry area, they are:

  • black
  • white
  • none

By turning on the Double tap enabled button you can enable or disable the double tap to zoom gesture. This will toggle between fully zoomed in and out.

Unlike most image layer types, when building your final issue the zoom image layer type will not shrink your zoomable image below the required fully zoomed in size.
