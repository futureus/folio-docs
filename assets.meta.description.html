Writing your app description

App descriptions are automatically collapsed roughly after the first paragraph, so ideally in that space you will want to sell your app's functionality to the best of your ability.

Your description is plain text, however the Apple emoji icons still work if you know how to use them and wish to add to your description.

In the app review process the description will be read and you can fail on the data entered here alone. You cannot infringe on copyrights in the space or claim any relation to Apple either directly or indirectly (i.e. through a competition prize).
