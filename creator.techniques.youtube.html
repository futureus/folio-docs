Playing YouTube videos

YouTube videos are embedded in a special way, requiring HTML to activate their play controls. To avoid complication, FutureFolio Creator has a special method of embedding YouTube videos that makes it much easier: create a story layer, then paste your YouTube URL into the Source File box. FutureFolio will recognise this link and wrap it inside the correct YouTube HTML to make it function correctly.

Do not use youtu.be

FutureFolio looks for URLs that begin with "http://www.youtube.". This means you should not use YouTube's URL shortening service, youtu.be.
