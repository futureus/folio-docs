Curves

Your animation curve dictates how your movements happen on the page. There are four options, and the default is "both":

  • linear - your animation will happen at a constant speed.
  • start - your animation will start slowly, then pick up speed.
  • end - your animation will start at full speed, then slow down.
  • both - your animation will start at slowly, pick up speed, then slow down.

The "both" curve gives an S-curve of acceleration and deceleration and is the most visually pleasing.
