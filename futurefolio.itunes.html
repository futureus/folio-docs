Importing issues via iTunes

You can quickly and easily preview issues on your iPad/iPhone by connecting it to your Mac and using iTunes.

Open iTunes, click on your device in the library tab on the left and on the main summary pane select the Apps tab. Scroll down the page till you see the File Sharing section. This has a list of Apps that have file sharing enabled. Select FutureFolio Viewer and drag an issue zip into that pane.

Once copying is complete, open FutureFolio Viewer on your device and tap Add Issues when prompted.
